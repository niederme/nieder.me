Hi, I'm John Niedermeyer, a designer based in New York.

My work has spanned startups, newsrooms, and scaled platforms, shaping products and brands at the intersection of technology, media, and culture. Most recently, I was Principal Designer at Resy, working across the product suite from Consumer to Restaurant, helping shape experiences across the ecosystem. Before that, as Head of Design, I led Product, Brand, Experiential, and Editorial design through the company's post-Series A growth.

Originally from Buffalo, N.Y., I started my career in Boston and have spent most of the past two decades living and working in New York City. Earlier on, I spent nearly eight years at The New York Times, with roles spanning the Newsroom, Opinion, Elections, and Product.
