SendMoi

Accessibility Statement

Effective date: March 5, 2026

SendMoi is intended to be usable by as many people as possible, including people using assistive technologies.

Our Commitment

We aim to make both the SendMoi app and this website accessible and straightforward to use. Accessibility is part of how we design, build, and update SendMoi.

How We Approach Accessibility

We work toward accessibility best practices for each platform we support, including Apple's accessibility guidance for apps and WCAG-aligned practices for the web.

For the SendMoi website, we focus on semantic HTML, readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, and reduced-motion support where appropriate.

Known Limitations

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and some areas may not yet work perfectly for every user or assistive technology combination.

If you run into a barrier, please share as much detail as possible so we can investigate and improve it.

Feedback and Support

We welcome accessibility feedback, bug reports, and requests for help.

Email us at mailmoi@nieder.me with:

  • what you were trying to do,
  • what device, OS, and browser or app version you were using, and
  • what assistive technology you were using (if any).

Statement Updates

This statement may be updated as SendMoi evolves. When it changes, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated.

Contact

For accessibility questions, contact mailmoi@nieder.me.
