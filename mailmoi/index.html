MailMoi

Send anything to yourself.

MailMoi makes it easy to send links to yourself without losing them in tabs, bookmarks, or chats.

Share with MailMoi

Share with MailMoi from the system sheet, and it arrives as a polished email instead of getting lost in tabs, bookmarks, or chats.

Your Gmail, kept private

It sends through your own Gmail account. MailMoi stays local-first, with no backend storing your session, recipients, or queued mail.

Readable when it lands

The result is easy to scan: a clean subject, useful context, and a source link that still feels good on both phone and desktop.

Offline queue

If the network is flaky, the send is not dropped. MailMoi keeps it in a local queue and retries when the app wakes up or connectivity returns.

Recent recipients

Keep a default recipient ready, then switch fast with recent-recipient shortcuts when something needs to go somewhere else.
