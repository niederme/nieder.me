MailMoi Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MailMoi handles information when you use the app.

Information MailMoi Handles

MailMoi is designed to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

  • Gmail account information: When you sign in with Google, MailMoi receives your Gmail email address and the OAuth tokens needed to keep you signed in and send email through your Gmail account.
  • Content you provide: MailMoi handles the recipient, title, excerpt, and URL you enter in the app or share into the app through the Share Extension.
  • Queued email data: If an email cannot be sent immediately, MailMoi stores the queued email locally on your device until it can retry.
  • Saved preferences: MailMoi stores your default recipient and recent recipients locally so the app and Share Extension can reuse them.
  • Support communications: If you contact us directly, we may receive your name, email address, and any information you include in your message.

How Information Is Used

Any information handled by MailMoi is used only to:

  • sign you in with Google,
  • send email through the Gmail API on your behalf,
  • save and retry queued emails,
  • remember your default and recent recipients,
  • fetch page metadata or preview images for links you choose to share,
  • respond to support requests,
  • diagnose app issues when you report them, and
  • meet legal obligations if required.

Where Data Is Stored

MailMoi stores app data locally on your device. Depending on the feature, this may include the system keychain, app storage, and shared app-group storage used by the MailMoi Share Extension.

MailMoi does not run its own backend service for account storage, syncing, analytics, or email delivery.

What Is Sent Off Your Device

When MailMoi makes network requests, they are sent directly from your device to the services required for the feature you are using.

  • Google: Used for authentication and Gmail delivery.
  • Websites you share: If you share a link, MailMoi may request that page and its preview image to improve the email content.
  • Apple platform services: MailMoi may use Apple system frameworks to run the app and, where available on your device, generate an on-device summary of shared article content.

MailMoi does not sell your personal information and does not use your data for advertising or user profiling.

Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. You can also sign out of Gmail inside the app, delete queued emails before they are sent, and change or clear your saved default recipient at any time. If you want to request deletion of support communications, contact us using the email address below.

Children’s Privacy

MailMoi is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. When it changes, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated.
