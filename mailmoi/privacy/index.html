Mailmoi Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mailmoi handles information when you use the app.

Information Mailmoi Collects

Mailmoi is designed to collect as little information as possible.

  • Content you provide: If you choose to submit text, links, or other material through the app, that information may be processed in order to provide the app’s features.
  • Support communications: If you contact us directly, we may receive your name, email address, and any information you include in your message.
  • Basic technical data: The app or its hosting infrastructure may automatically log limited technical details such as device type, app version, and error information for reliability and debugging.

How Information Is Used

Any information handled by Mailmoi is used only to:

  • operate the app’s core functionality,
  • respond to support requests,
  • diagnose bugs and improve reliability, and
  • meet legal obligations if required.

Data Sharing

Mailmoi does not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only with service providers that help operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, or error-reporting tools, and only to the extent needed for those services to function.

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, resolve support issues, maintain security, or comply with legal requirements.

Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. If you want to request deletion of support communications or other directly submitted information, contact us using the email address below.

Children’s Privacy

Mailmoi is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. When it changes, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated.

Contact
