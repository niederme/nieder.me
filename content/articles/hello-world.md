This is the first post on **NiederBlog** — a place for shorter writing alongside the
longer-form [case studies](/work/). It runs on the same static foundation as the rest
of nieder.me: Markdown in Git, rendered to plain HTML at build time, deployed as inert
files. No server, no database.

## Why now

Case studies are deliberate and infrequent. A blog gives room for the in-between thinking:
process notes, craft details, and the small decisions that don't warrant a full case study
but are worth writing down.
